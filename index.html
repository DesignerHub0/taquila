OUR STORY

We are an ADDITIVE-FREE Premium Tequila 100 % Natural made in honor to our father Don Ramón González from Barranca de Santa Clara Jalisco, in collaboration with la Autentica Tequilera distillery NOM 1502, owned by Orendain family, prestige and tradition since 1926.

Our pasion and love for blue agave and real tequila was passed down by our father Don Ramon Gonzalez who like many immigrants in the United States, he worked tirelessly to achieve the “American Dream” aspiring to return one day to his hometown to fulfill his mission. Unfortunately, death came early, leaving us with the task to continue, a responsibility we've carried out with utmost dedication. Thus, Tequila RG Legado was born, aiming to distill a premium tequila with rich flavor and exquisite smoothness.

Our brand is proudly 100% Mexican-owned, with corporate offices in Los Angeles, California. The tequila is imported from Tequila, Jalisco, Mexico, through Mara Imports.

UNIQUE PROCESS

  • - Region/ Blue Agawaves from Jalisco,50% Valley and 50% Highlands
  • - Water Source / Clean Spring Water
  • - Cooking / Low-pressure Autoclaves
  • - Extraction of juice with a Roller Miller
  • - Zero-Waste distillery due to upcycling of the Bagasse
  • - Fermentation 100% Natural
  • - Double Distillation
  • - Aging Free Brand Member / Tequilla Match Maker

TEQUILAS

AÑEJO CRISTALINO

18 MONTHS

After aging our tequila in American Oak Barrels of Bourbon (whiskey) for 18 months, our exquisite Añejo Tequila was charcoal filtration process to remove color and enhance smoothness, without the need for any additives or flavorings.

REPOSADO

8 MONTHS

The sweet and woody flavor of our reposado derives from the aging process. By using medium toast, white oak burbon barrels the tequila is enfused with its rich flavors.
